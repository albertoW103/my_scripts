# render a video from vmd


1. load your trayectory file.
2. set format for your video.
3. go to Extensions/Visualization/Movie Maker
4. set following options:
- Renderer: POV-Ray/Tachion (best quality)
- Movie settings: Trayectory
- Format: Animated gif

Others:
Trayectory setp size: 1
Movie duration (seconds): 15
